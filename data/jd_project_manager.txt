Job Title: Project Manager
Location: Delhi, India (Hybrid)
Employment Type: Full-time
Experience Required: 5-8 years

About the Role:
We are looking for an experienced Project Manager to lead cross-functional initiatives and drive successful project delivery. You will be responsible for planning, executing, and closing projects while managing stakeholders, budgets, timelines, and risks. This role requires strong leadership, communication, and organizational skills.

Key Responsibilities:
- Define project scope, goals, and deliverables in collaboration with stakeholders
- Develop detailed project plans, schedules, and budgets
- Manage project resources and allocate tasks across team members
- Track project progress and report status to stakeholders
- Identify and mitigate project risks and issues
- Facilitate meetings, stand-ups, and retrospectives
- Ensure projects are delivered on time, within scope, and within budget
- Manage vendor relationships and third-party deliverables
- Drive continuous improvement in project management processes
- Maintain project documentation and lessons learned

Must-Have Skills:
- Project planning and scheduling
- Stakeholder management and communication
- Risk and issue management
- Budget management and resource allocation
- Agile and Scrum methodologies
- JIRA or equivalent project management tools
- MS Project or similar planning tools
- Team leadership and facilitation
- Problem-solving and decision making
- Vendor management

Nice-to-Have Skills:
- PMP or PRINCE2 certification
- Scrum Master certification (CSM, PSM)
- Experience with SAFe or other scaled agile frameworks
- Technical background or understanding of software development
- Experience with data analytics or AI projects
- Change management experience

Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Business, Engineering, or related field
- PMP certification strongly preferred
- 5+ years of project management experience
- Excellent negotiation and conflict resolution skills

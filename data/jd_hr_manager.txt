Job Title: HR Manager
Location: Bangalore, India (On-site)
Employment Type: Full-time
Experience Required: 7-12 years

About the Role:
We are looking for an experienced HR Manager to lead our human resources department. You will be responsible for overseeing all HR functions including talent acquisition, employee relations, performance management, compensation and benefits, compliance, and organizational development. This is a strategic role that requires both operational excellence and people leadership.

Key Responsibilities:
- Develop and implement HR strategies aligned with business objectives
- Lead the talent acquisition team to attract and retain top talent
- Manage employee relations, grievance handling, and conflict resolution
- Design and administer compensation and benefits programs
- Ensure compliance with all labor laws and regulations
- Drive performance management processes including goal setting, reviews, and feedback
- Champion diversity, equity, and inclusion initiatives
- Oversee HR operations, HRMS systems, and HR analytics
- Manage organizational development and change management initiatives
- Develop and deliver training programs for employee upskilling

Must-Have Skills:
- HR strategy and operations
- Talent acquisition and recruitment
- Employee relations management
- Performance management systems
- Compensation and benefits administration
- Labor law and compliance
- HR analytics and reporting
- Communication and interpersonal skills
- Leadership and team management
- Conflict resolution

Nice-to-Have Skills:
- HRMS implementation experience (SAP SuccessFactors, Workday)
- Organization development certification
- Diversity and inclusion program management
- Change management certification
- Experience with startups or high-growth companies

Qualifications:
- Master's degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or related field
- SHRM-SCP or SPHR certification preferred
- 7+ years of progressive HR experience with at least 3 years in a managerial role

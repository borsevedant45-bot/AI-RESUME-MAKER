Job Title: Mid-Level Data Analyst
Location: Mumbai, India (Hybrid)
Employment Type: Full-time
Experience Required: 3-6 years

About the Role:
We are seeking a skilled Data Analyst to join our analytics team. You will work with large datasets to uncover insights, build dashboards, and support data-driven decision making across the organization. You will collaborate with product, marketing, and engineering teams to identify opportunities and drive business impact through data.

Key Responsibilities:
- Analyze complex datasets to identify trends, patterns, and actionable insights
- Build and maintain dashboards and reports using visualization tools
- Write efficient SQL queries to extract and transform data
- Conduct A/B testing and statistical analysis to inform product decisions
- Present findings and recommendations to stakeholders
- Collaborate with data engineers to improve data quality and accessibility
- Develop and maintain documentation for analytics processes
- Support ad-hoc analysis requests from business teams

Must-Have Skills:
- SQL (advanced querying, window functions, CTEs)
- Data visualization (Tableau, Power BI, or Looker)
- Python for data analysis (pandas, numpy, matplotlib)
- Statistical analysis and hypothesis testing
- Excel (advanced formulas, pivot tables)
- Data storytelling and presentation
- Business acumen and problem-solving
- Attention to detail

Nice-to-Have Skills:
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
- ETL pipeline knowledge
- Experience with dbt or similar transformation tools
- R programming
- Machine learning basics
- Experience with product analytics tools (Amplitude, Mixpanel)

Qualifications:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science, Economics, or related quantitative field
- 3-6 years of experience in data analysis or business intelligence
- Strong written and verbal communication skills

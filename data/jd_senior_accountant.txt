Job Title: Senior Accountant
Location: Chennai, India (On-site)
Employment Type: Full-time
Experience Required: 6-10 years

About the Role:
We are seeking a detail-oriented Senior Accountant to join our finance team. You will oversee the day-to-day accounting operations, ensure accuracy of financial records, manage month-end close processes, and support financial reporting and compliance. This role requires deep knowledge of accounting principles, tax regulations, and internal controls.

Key Responsibilities:
- Manage the full accounting cycle including accounts payable, accounts receivable, and general ledger
- Prepare and review monthly, quarterly, and annual financial statements
- Oversee month-end and year-end close processes
- Ensure compliance with Indian GAAP, Ind AS, and tax regulations (GST, TDS, income tax)
- Coordinate with external auditors for statutory audits
- Manage cash flow forecasting and bank reconciliations
- Prepare tax returns and ensure timely filing of all statutory requirements
- Develop and maintain internal control policies and procedures
- Supervise and mentor junior accounting staff
- Support budgeting and financial planning activities

Must-Have Skills:
- Indian GAAP and Ind AS accounting standards
- Financial statement preparation and analysis
- GST, TDS, and direct tax compliance
- Month-end and year-end close processes
- Internal controls and audit support
- Advanced Excel (pivot tables, VLOOKUP, financial modeling)
- Tally ERP or SAP FI
- Accounts payable and receivable management
- Bank reconciliation
- Attention to detail and accuracy

Nice-to-Have Skills:
- CA (Chartered Accountant) or CPA qualification
- Experience with ERP implementation
- Knowledge of IFRS
- Experience in manufacturing or technology sectors
- Process improvement and automation experience
- Advanced financial modeling

Qualifications:
- Chartered Accountant (CA) or MBA Finance with strong academic record
- 6-10 years of progressive accounting experience
- Proven experience supervising teams
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
